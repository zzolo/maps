Public Art in San Francisco

The map
Pan around the map to search for art around San Francisco. Roll over points to see the title of art, and click on the points to get all the information about the art pieces.

The player
Use the player controls in the top right to fly around the map. As you fly through you will see the title and an image of the art if it's available. Use the stop button to remove the overlay.

Data
All the data on this map is open, public data from the city of San Francisco found at datasf.org. Data including elevation lines, parks and open spaces, bodies of water, and public art.
